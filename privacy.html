Privacy Policy

At CreatorToolkit, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Browser-Based Tools

CreatorToolkit provides browser-based tools for YouTube creators. All processing for our tools (like Thumbnail Maker, Video Title Generator, etc.) happens locally in your browser. We do not store your video data, titles, or images on our servers.

2. No User Accounts

We do not require user accounts or registrations. You can use all our tools anonymously without providing any personal information.

3. Cookies and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our site. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

4. Third-Party Data Collection

Third-party ad providers may collect data such as your IP address, browser type, and interaction with ads for analytical and targeting purposes. This data collection is governed by the respective privacy policies of those third-party providers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us via the information provided on our About page.
